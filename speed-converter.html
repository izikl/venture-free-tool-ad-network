Frequently Asked Questions

How many km/h is 1 mph?
1 mph = 1.60934 km/h.
How fast is 1 knot in km/h?
1 knot = 1.852 km/h. Knots are used in maritime and aviation.
What is Mach 1?
Mach 1 is the speed of sound, approximately 1234.8 km/h (767 mph) at sea level.
