Frequently Asked Questions

How many joules in a calorie?
1 calorie (small calorie) = 4.184 joules. 1 kilocalorie (food calorie) = 4,184 joules.
How many joules in a kWh?
1 kWh = 3,600,000 joules (3.6 MJ). This is the amount of energy used by a 1,000-watt device in 1 hour.
What is a BTU?
A BTU (British Thermal Unit) = 1,055.06 joules. It is the energy needed to raise 1 lb of water by 1°F. Commonly used for HVAC systems.
