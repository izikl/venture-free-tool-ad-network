Frequently Asked Questions

How many pounds in a kilogram?
1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds. Multiply kilograms by 2.20462 to get pounds.
How many grams in an ounce?
1 ounce = 28.3495 grams.
What is a stone in kg?
1 stone = 6.35029 kilograms (14 pounds). Stones are used in the UK and Ireland for body weight.
