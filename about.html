About UnitKit

UnitKit provides free, accurate unit conversion tools for everyday and professional use. Our tools cover length, weight, temperature, volume, speed, area, data storage, time, fuel economy, cooking measurements, pressure, and energy.

All conversions are performed instantly in your browser — no sign-up required, no data collected.
