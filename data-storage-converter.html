Frequently Asked Questions

How many bytes in a kilobyte?
1 KB = 1,000 bytes (SI definition). However, 1 KiB (kibibyte) = 1,024 bytes.
How many MB in a GB?
1 GB = 1,000 MB (SI). In binary: 1 GiB = 1,024 MiB.
What is the difference between GB and GiB?
GB (gigabyte) uses base-10: 1 GB = 10^9 bytes. GiB (gibibyte) uses base-2: 1 GiB = 2^30 = 1,073,741,824 bytes.
