Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between US MPG and UK MPG?
US MPG uses the US gallon (3.785 L), while UK/Imperial MPG uses the Imperial gallon (4.546 L). A car with 30 US MPG = 36.04 UK MPG.
How do I convert MPG to L/100km?
Formula: L/100km = 235.214 ÷ MPG (US). So 30 MPG = 7.84 L/100km.
What is good fuel economy?
For a typical car: 30+ MPG (US) / under 8 L/100km is considered good. Hybrids and EVs can achieve 50+ MPGe.
