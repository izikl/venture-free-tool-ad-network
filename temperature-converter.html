Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?
Formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32. Example: 25°C = (25 × 1.8) + 32 = 77°F.
How do I convert Fahrenheit to Celsius?
Formula: °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9. Example: 98.6°F = (98.6 − 32) × 5/9 = 37°C.
What is absolute zero in Celsius and Fahrenheit?
Absolute zero is 0 K = −273.15°C = −459.67°F. It is the lowest theoretically possible temperature.
