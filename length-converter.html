Frequently Asked Questions

How many feet are in a meter?
1 meter = 3.28084 feet. So to convert meters to feet, multiply by 3.28084.
How many centimeters are in an inch?
1 inch = 2.54 centimeters. To convert inches to centimeters, multiply by 2.54.
How do I convert miles to kilometers?
1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. Multiply the number of miles by 1.60934 to get kilometers.
What is a nautical mile?
A nautical mile is 1,852 meters (about 1.151 regular miles). It is used in maritime and aviation navigation.
